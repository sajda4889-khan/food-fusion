French Fries

Ingredients

  • Potatoes : 500 g
  • Oil : 300 ml
  • Salt : 5 g
  • Black pepper : 2 g

steps of making

  1. Peel and cut 500 g potatoes into thin strips.
  2. Wash and dry properly.
  3. Heat 300 ml oil in a pan.
  4. Fry potatoes until golden.
  5. Remove and drain excess oil.
  6. Add salt and pepper.

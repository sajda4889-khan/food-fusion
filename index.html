Biryani

Ingredients

  • Rice : 300 kg
  • Chicken : 500 g
  • Yogurt : 100 g
  • Onion : 150 g
  • Tomato : 100 g
  • Ginger garlic paste : 20 g
  • Biryani masala : 15 g
  • Oil : 50 ml
  • Salt : 10 g

steps of making

  1. Wash and soak 300 g rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry 150 g sliced onions in 50 ml oil until golden.
  3. Add 500 g chicken and cook for 5 to 7 minutes
  4. Add 20 g ginger garlic paste and 100 g tomatoes.
  5. Add 100 g yogurt and 15 g biryani masala.
  6. Cook until chicken is tender.
  7. Boil rice until 70% cooked.
  8. Layer rice and chicken mixture.
  9. Cook on low heat for 15 to 20 minutes.

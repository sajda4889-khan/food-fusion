Chocolate Milkshake

Ingredients

  • Milk :250 ml
  • Chocolate syrup : 30 ml
  • Sugar :20 g
  • Ice cream :50 g
  • Ice cubes : 5 pieces

steps of making

  1. Add 250 ml milk into a blender.
  2. Add 30 ml chocolate syrup and 20 g sugar.
  3. Add ice cream and ice cubes.
  4. Blend for 30 to 40 seconds.
  5. Pour into a glass.

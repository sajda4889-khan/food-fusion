Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • Flour :200 g
  • Cocoa powder : 50 g
  • Sugar : 150 g
  • Eggs : 2 pieces (about 100 g)
  • Butter : 100 g
  • Baking powder : 5 g
  • Milk : 200 ml

steps of making

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix 200 g flour, 50 g cocoa powder, and 5 g baking powder.
  3. Beat 100 g butter with 150 g sugar.
  4. Add 2 eggs and mix well.
  5. Add dry mixture and 200 ml milk slowly.
  6. Pour into baking tray.
  7. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes.
  8. Let it cool before serving.
